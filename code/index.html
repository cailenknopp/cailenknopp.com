About me

Hi there. My name is Cailen Knopp and it would appear you’ve stumbled upon my website (perhaps even on perhaps!). In any case of your arrival, welcome. This is my biography.

I am not really sure what to write for my biography. I’m only sixteen years old. In the time in which I am not confined to my under-renovation high school, I’m usually curling. Traveling around the southernmost region of my province and playing against other curlers in the hope of winning a small pool of money has kind of become the norm. Sometimes I even win! Curling is a big part of my life and such I budget a lot of time doing so. Another use of my time outside of school and school related work is computers (I know ‘computers’ is a noun and not a verb but just stay with me). I like to learn new programming languages and make little program things to run on my computer. One day, two years ago, I made my computer say Hello World, and since then I’ve been making it say other things. You can check out my GitHub here and I will put some of my code up there (however, I many not be bothered to).
I hope you enjoyed my biography, thanks for reading.
